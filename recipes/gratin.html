Gratin

Description:

This gratin is my favourite dish that mom makes, it is satiated with melted white creamy cheese that blows your tasting buds away. The shell or butterfly shaped pasta are as important as the cheese, because design is an essential aspect in this recipe alongside its delicious taste. It is best served with chicken inside, as the salty rich flavour of it plays a huge role in exploding tatses in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Fresh Cream
  • Mushroom
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken,then put it aside
  2. Pour fresh cream in a pot and apply heat for 30 mins
  3. Boil the pasta in a pot filled with water, and add a tablespoon of salt
  4. Add everything together in a big pan and spread mozarella on top with some mushrooms, then put in oven for 40 minutes
